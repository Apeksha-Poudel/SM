About SM Analyzer

SM Analyzer is an innovative AI-powered platform designed to assist investors in making informed decisions by analyzing and predicting stock market trends with unparalleled accuracy. Developed during the Turboline Hackathon in August 2024 (https://turboline.ai/hackathon/), SM Analyzer harnesses the power of advanced algorithms and OpenAI APIs to provide comprehensive insights into market dynamics.

Our product offers two modes of analysis: Basic Analyzer and Advance Analyzer. The Basic Analyzer caters to beginners, offering an intuitive interface with essential metrics, while the Advance Analyzer is designed for seasoned investors, providing detailed financial indicators and technical analysis tools.

Key Features:

  • Select and analyze stocks from various global exchanges.
  • Input and evaluate critical financial metrics such as P/E ratio, book value, and RSI.
  • Advanced options including Bollinger Bands, Alpha/Beta values, and more for detailed analysis.
  • Upload chart screenshots for AI-driven pattern recognition and analysis.
  • Toggle between basic and advanced modes to suit your investment style.
  • Receive AI-generated predictions to guide your investment strategies.

We would like to extend our gratitude to Turboline (https://turboline.ai/) for providing us with the platform and support to bring SM Analyzer to life.
