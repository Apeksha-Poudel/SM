Welcome to SM Analyzer

SM Analyzer is an AI-powered platform designed to assist investors in analyzing and predicting stock market trends with precision. By leveraging advanced algorithms and OpenAI APIs, our tool provides comprehensive insights based on key financial metrics, helping you make informed investment decisions. Whether you are a beginner or a seasoned investor, SM Analyzer offers both basic and advanced features to suit your needs.

Key Features:

  • Select stock exchange and input company symbol for analysis.
  • Evaluate key metrics like P/E ratio, book value, RSI, and moving averages.
  • Advanced options include Bollinger Bands, Alpha/Beta values, and more.
  • Upload chart screenshots for detailed pattern analysis.
  • Toggle between basic and advanced investing modes easily.
  • Receive AI-generated predictions to guide your investment strategy.
